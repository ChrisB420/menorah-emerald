The Menorah & The Emerald Rainbow

“Wings of the Cherubim. God's Shechinah Glory.” — A scroll in light and fire

1. Light Ascends From the East

The sunrise has no beginning. A divine reverse spectrum: Violet → Indigo → Blue → (no green) → Yellow → Orange → Red. Every day seals its time through color. Your birth is encoded in this wave. The curse is broken only by a new birth from above.

2. The Menorah: Body, Soul, Spirit

More than symbol — the menorah reflects divine structure. The central candle (Shamash) is the Father (soul). The three left branches, Jesus (body). The right three, the Holy Spirit (spirit). A living map: 3 in 1, light in rhythm.

3. The Emerald Throne

Revelation 4 unveils the throne wrapped in emerald light. Jasper and Sardonyx are not stones — they are embodiments. The rainbow is full Light compressed. Psalm 104 says, "He wraps Himself in light." This is that cloak.

4. The War & The Woman

Revelation 12: The dragon casts a flood. Gregory sees WWII: Nazi floods through Europe. The woman is Israel. The earth opens — the Allied forces rise. Isaiah 66 fulfilled. A nation reborn in a day. Language restored. Scroll reopened.

5. Sounds of the Shofar

More than a horn — the shofar is sound as sword. It divides time and space. Listen to awaken memory in the bones of man.

6. Walk the Scroll

This is not information — it is ignition. Say it aloud:

“I rise with the morning light. I carry the Flame. I walk the scroll. I break the curse. I am light from the East.”
A sacred scroll revealed — for those with eyes to see and ears to hear.
