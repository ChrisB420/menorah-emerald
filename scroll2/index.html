Scroll 2: The War in Heaven

“And there was war in Heaven...” — Revelation 12

1. The Dragon’s Flood

The enemy unleashed a flood. Gregory saw it in WWII — Nazi Germany as the beast rising. A force possessed, marching east through Europe. The woman — Israel — was the target.

2. The Earth Opened Her Mouth

The Allied forces arose. America, Britain, and the underground answered the cry. Like the earth itself opened and swallowed the waters of death. This was prophecy, not just politics.

3. Isaiah 66 Fulfilled

“Can a nation be born in a day?” It was. In 1948, Israel rose again — reborn, re-spoken, and restored in ancient tongue. Hebrew revived. God’s clock restarted. The scroll reopened.

4. The Dragon Cast Down

Revelation 12 tells it straight. Satan falls — and great wrath comes to earth. He knows his time is short. The warfare now is not just global — it is personal, generational, and dimensional.

5. Your Position in the War

You are not bystander. Your birth was timed. Your awakening matters. This scroll is your signal: rise. Overcome by the blood of the Lamb, the word of your testimony, and not loving your life unto death.

“We are the remnant. We are the warriors. We are the ones who carry the scrolls of memory into battle.”
